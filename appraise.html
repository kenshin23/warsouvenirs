We can appraise your war souvenirs by viewing your high-resolution pictures for a modest fee, as per the schedule listed below. ( Prices are in US dollars )

After receiving the appraisal, if you decide you would like to sell your items to the Midwest Military Museum, the fee would be waived or refunded.

PROCEDURE

You can choose to send your photos through our "CONTACT US" link on our home page. You can expedite appraisal by paying through our PAYPAL link.

Or, if you wish, you could send photos and a check (money orders preferred) to cover appraisal fees to:

Midwest Military Museum,
P. O. Box 682,
Lake Zurich, IL 60047

Please remember to include your contact information plus phone number if using the mail-in method.

Additionally, if you are in the Chicago or suburban area, a confidential house call can be arranged at a time that is convenient for you.

Please Note
We are totally involved in the preservation of history and historical artifacts.
We are in no way affiliated with nor do we endorse any group or movement that is connected with subversive ideology (i.e. White Power, Nazi, etc.).
